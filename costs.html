⚠ Cost Insight — Streaming Minutes dominate
99.8% of the Foundry Tools cost is streaming time, not TTS usage. The avatar stream is always-on once a session starts — idle pose costs the same as speaking. To reduce costs consider: (1) adding a session timeout that calls synthesizer.close() after N minutes of inactivity, or (2) lazy-init the avatar only on first user interaction instead of on page load.
